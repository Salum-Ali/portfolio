Considering I had experience with poster design and wanted to try a diferent style, I bought myself my first drawing tablet. In order to practice using it, I decided to do project-based work with deadlines, focusing on Environment and Prop Design.
As there where eight months left in the year I decided to create one Environment (and maybe prop) -focused piece every month for the last eight months of the year. The name was also partially insprired by the Endless Eight arc of the anime The Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumiya, hence Environment Eight.
